Full Simulation

Best for a realistic TOEIC report with full timing and proctoring checks before entering the test runner.

  • Requires active TOEIC package access.
  • Uses camera setup before the test starts.
  • Produces Listening, Reading, and total score report.

Practice Simulation

Best for preparation and repetition without the full simulation pressure.

  • Uses `mode=prep` route.
  • Can start directly from instructions.
  • Produces practice score and part feedback.

Rules

Keep the rules visible so students understand what could invalidate a full simulation.

  • Stay in the test window during proctored sessions.
  • Keep camera and microphone permissions active.
  • Do not refresh, close, or switch tabs during the test.
